Needs assessment email

The following email will be sent to the student on completion of this stage of the application:

Dear Liam Bloggs,

I can confirm the NHS will fund the cost of a DSAs study needs assessment to determine the level of support you will need to undertake your course.

Arranging a Needs Assessment

These can be carried out by a DSA-QAG registered or accredited assessment centre. An up-to-date list of accredited assessment centres can be found at the following link: DSA-QAG.

Uploading the Report

On completion of the study needs assessment, a copy of your assessment report should be uploaded to your portal.

The Needs Assessment fee

Funding theough DSAs usually cover the cost of one full DSA study needs assessment. There may be circumstances where we agree another assessment is necessary.

The fee for the Needs Assessment will be met through the DSAs on your behalf.

Other information

If you have any queries about your DSA study needs assessment, the Disability Officer at your university will be able to assist you.

If you need to contact us about your DSA application, our student helpline telephone number is 0300 330 1345. If you prefer to email us, the address is nhsbsa.sbaccount@nhs.net
