WIP: What type of independent student are you?

You are an independent if you:

  • have any dependent children at the start of the academic year
  • have no living biological or adoptive parents
  • are estranged from your parents
What classes as a dependent child

A dependent child is a person aged:

  • 0 to 15 in a household (whether they are in a family or not)
  • 16 to 18 in full-time education and living in a family with his or her parent(s) or grandparent(s) and not undertaking an apprenticeship/receiving a full time wage

It does not include any people aged 16 to 18 who have a spouse, partner or child living in the household.

What classes as being estranged from your parents

This only includes your biological or adoptive parents.

To apply as an estranged student, we expect that you've had no contact with either of your parents in the 12 months before the first day of your course.
