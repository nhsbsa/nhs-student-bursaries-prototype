Your income

What you will need

To assess your child's NHS Bursary application, we need to know how much you earned in the last tax year.

We'll ask you if you got money from:

  • a job or self-employment
  • bank or building society interest
  • sponsorship
  • rent (full property or part of your home)
  • a pension or taxable state benefits
  • maintenance payments

Evidence you will need

Depending on your answers, you may need to upload copies of your:

  • payslips
  • P60, P45, or a letter from your employer
  • tax return, self-assessment form, or a letter from your accountant
  • bank statements
  • rent or mortgage statements
  • most recent pension statement
  • HMRC or benefits letter
  • rent or mortgage statements
  • child maintenance letter

Important

You will need to provide information for the most recently completed financial year. Please ensure the values you declare match the supporting evidence you provide exactly.

Information:

For your security, you will be signed out of the service if you do not do anything for 30 minutes.
