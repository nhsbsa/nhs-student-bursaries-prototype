What you’ll need

To make sure we assess you on your income correctly, we need to know about any expenses you have in the academic year you're submitting a change for.

You need to tell us about:

  • life assurance payments
  • maintenance payments

You may need to upload evidence to support some of your answers. Examples of evidence include:

  • payslips
  • life assurance policies
  • bank statements
  • rent statements
  • Child Maintenance Service letters
