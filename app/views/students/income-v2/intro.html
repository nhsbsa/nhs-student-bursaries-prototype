Your incomeWhat you’ll need

To make sure we assess you on your income correctly, we need to know about money you'll get in the academic year you're submitting a change for.

You need to tell us about:

  • sponsorships
  • pension payments
  • rental income
  • taxable state benefits
  • maintenance payments
  • trust funds
  • other taxable income

You also need to tell us about any pension payments you got in the last financial year and any employment or self-employment income if you’re a part-time student.

Evidence you’ll need

You may need to upload evidence to support some of your answers. Examples of evidence include:

  • payslips
  • pension statements
  • tax returns
  • benefits letters
  • Child Maintenance Service letters
Information:

For your security, you’ll be signed out if you do nothing for 60 minutes.
