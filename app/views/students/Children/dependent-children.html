Dependent children Do you have any dependent children?

A dependent child is a person aged:

  • 0 to 15 in a household (whether they are in a family or not)
  • 16 to 18 in full-time education and living in a family with his or her parent(s) or grandparent(s)

It does not include any people aged 16 to 18 who have a spouse, partner or child living in the household.
