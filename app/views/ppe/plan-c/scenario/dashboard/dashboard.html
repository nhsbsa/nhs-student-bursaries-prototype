Welcome, John Smith Your travel expenses

Before you start your placement claim

You must enter the elements which apply to you in each section. These are manageable lists that you can keep up to date throughout the year and this is where your claim will pull the different elements of your claim from.

  • University travel

    This is your normal daily travel journey to university.

  • Placement addresses

    Enter the addresses of all placement sites you will attend

  • Accommodation addresses

    If you are staying away from your usual term time accommodation enter the addresses of any other accommodation you will be using during a placement.

  • Placement journeys

    This uses your placement and accommodation addresses to create details of the journeys you have undertaken as part of your placement.

  • Passengers

    Enter details of any other NHS Bursary funded passengers who travelled with you in your own vehicle to your placement.

  • Start claim

    Only when you are happy you have entered all relevant information to build your claim should you click start claim. This will take you through a process that builds your expenses claim using the information you have already entered. You will be able to pause, save and come back to your claim as often as you wish before submission.
