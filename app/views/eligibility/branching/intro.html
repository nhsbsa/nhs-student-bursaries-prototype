Before you start What you will need

To qualify for an NHS bursary, you must have:

  • the right to live in the UK
  • lived in the UK for more than 3 years before the first day of the academic year you’re applying for

Depending on your circumstances we’ll ask you:

  • about your residency status
  • where you’ve lived in the last 3 years or 5 years
  • to upload evidence of your right to live in the UK

Evidence you’ll have to provide

If you’re a UK National, you’ll need to upload a copy of your passport or birth certificate.

If you are not a UK National,you’ll need to upload a copy of your passport and one of the following:

  • UK residency card or permit
  • EU identity card

If you have applied for a student loan

If you're getting a loan from Student Finance England, we will not need to ask you any questions about your residency status.

Your loan must be from Student Finance England. We’ll ask you to upload evidence of your Student Finance England letter to prove this.
