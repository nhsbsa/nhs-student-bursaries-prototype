Privacy notice

The NHS Business Services Authority (NHSBSA) is responsible for this service.

We'll use the information you enter to:

  • access your applicaiton
  • pay you
  • detect and prevent fraud and mistakes
  • help plan and make inprovements to NHS services, and/or direct patient care

We only keep your data for seven years after your course finishes.

Why we process your information

We're required by law to process this information for the Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC )

Sharing your personal information

To prevent, detect and investigate fraud errors, we may share your information with:

  • Student Loans Company
  • HM Revenue and Customs
  • higher education institutions
  • The Home Office
  • organisations that you receive benefits, bursaries, grants, or support from
  • bodies acting on behalf of the above organisations

We may also share your information with:

  • DHSC to investigate and prosecute fraud, or any unlawful activity that affects the NHS
  • the Cabinet Office in relation to the National Fraud Initiative

We may also share annonymised information with DHSC to check it complies with equality law.

Information that identifies you will not be shared outside the European Economic Area.

Your rights

The information you enter will be managed as required by Data Protection law.

You have the right to:

  • receive a copy of the information we hold about you
  • request your information be changed if you believe it was not correct at the time you provided it
  • request that we delete your information if you think we're keeping it for longer than necessary
