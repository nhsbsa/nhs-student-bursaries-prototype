MFA (SMS) v2 MVP Last updated: 6 October 2021

The content has been updated by the business and final run through given.

MFA (SMS) v2 Business draft Last updated: 8 July 2021

Updates to the registration journey based on a prototype review held by the service design team. This will still remain in business draft for the time being.

MFA (SMS) v2 Business draft Last updated: 30 June 2021

Another demo was shown today and questions were raised around whether it could be made mandatory for students to opt in. The prototype reflects what has been agreed so far so is being placed back in business draft for the time being until we have the answer to that question.

MFA (SMS) v2 Prototype dev Last updated: 22 June 2021

A confirmation screen was added based on feedback to make it clear to students that they have successfully entered their One-Time Password (OTP).

MFA (SMS) v1 Prototype dev Last updated: 17 June 2021

An initial prototype was developed for the SMS option of the multi-factor authentication. To be demo'd to the team for feedback and suggestions.

User research will explore:

  • Users expectations for MFA from BSA
  • Users' preference for MFA option
  • HEI user thoughts on using personal phones for receiving SMS
