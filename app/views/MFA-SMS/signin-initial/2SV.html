Two-step verification settings

Why do I need this?

Passwords can get stolen - especially if you use the same password for multiple sites. Adding two-step verification means that even if your password gets stolen, your {{ serviceName }} account will remain secure.

How does it work?

After you turn on two-step verification for your account, signing in will be a little different:

  • You'll enter your password, as usual.
  • We'll send you an OTP (One-time password).
  • You'll enter the OTP, and complete your Sign-In.
