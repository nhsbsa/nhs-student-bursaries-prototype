Before you start What you will need

You can only apply for Disabled Students' Allowance (DSA) if you're in receipt of an NHS Bursary.

You only need to apply for DSA once during your course.

We'll ask for information about:

  • your disability
  • what disability equipment you own

Depending on your answers, we may ask you to send us evidence of:

  • a report from a doctor or specialist that explains how your disability impacts you
  • your education, health and care (EHC) plan (if applicable)

If you have received DSA before

If you have received DSA from Student Finance before, we'll use this information to assess how much you can get. You'll need to upload evidence of your study needs assessment.
