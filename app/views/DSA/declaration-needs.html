Disabled students’ allowance Declaration

I confirm that:

  • the information and supporting documents I’ve provided in this application are correct
  • I have provided details and evidence of all previous Disabled Students Allowance claims, where applicable.
  • I understand that if I give incorrect information, my funding may be refused or cancelled
  • I understand that I am liable for the cost of providing the necessary evidence to establish my disability in support of my DSA application.
  • I agree that NHSBSA Student Services can use my DSA to pay for a study needs assessment to determine my study support requirements
  • I understand that NHSBSA Student Services and my disability officer, DSA Needs Assessor and my DSA suppliers may exchange information about my DSA application where this is necessary to make sure I get the support I need.
  • I understand that if I do not enrol in training, I’ll need to repay any advance payments of DSA
  • I understand that my equipment can only be used for my course of study and that I am responsible for paying any repair costs.
  • I understand NHSBSA Student Services reserves the right to pay any suppliers of any approved equipment and support directly. I will be notified if NHSBSA Student Services decide to make payments directly to suppliers on my behalf.
  • I understand that if I get money from DSA to buy specialist equipment, I need to provide proof of purchase within one month of the request being made
  • I agree to inform NHSBSA Student Services of any change in my circumstances
  • I agree that if I’m overpaid, I’ll repay the overpayment
  • I understand and accept if I withhold information from NHSBSA Student Services and / or provide false or misleading information disciplinary action may be taken against me and I may be liable to criminal prosecution.
