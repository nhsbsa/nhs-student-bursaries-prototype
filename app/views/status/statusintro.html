Apply for an income assessed bursary

This section will determine if you’re an independent or dependent student.

If you are an independent student

We will not consider your parents income when we assess how much financial support you can get.

You are an independent student if you:

  • have no living parents
  • are married, in a civil partnership, seperated, divorced or widowed
  • care for a person under the age of 18
  • have not had any contact with your parents for 1 year before the start of the academic year you’re applying for
  • have supported yourself for at least 3 years before the start of your course

If none of these apply to you, we’ll assess you as a dependent student.

Student Finance England assessment

If Student Finance England have confirmed you're an independent, you can upload evidence to complete this section.

If you are a dependent student

We’ll assess your application based on your parent’s income and expenses for the last tax year. Your parents need to complete this themselves.

Evidence you will need

Depending on your circumstances, you might need to send proof of:

  • your latest award letter from SFE
  • your parent’s death certificates
  • your marriage or civil partnership certificate
  • your divorce decree or partners death certificate
  • your payslips, P60s, P45s or tax return
  • a letter from your employers or HMRC
  • a letter from your local council or care authority
  • your child or children’s birth certificates
