Tasklist rework: Delete application, new CYAs, and content tweaks (30/8/2024)

Summary

  • Issue: Students completing applications with incorrect data are currently unable to go back and change their answers before submission.
  • Agreed solution:
    • Allow student to delete their application, from the Tasklist page.
    • Also add 'Check Your Answers' pages for each section they have completed (accessible from the Tasklist), to allow them to check (but NOT edit) their changes, before submitting or deleting their application.
