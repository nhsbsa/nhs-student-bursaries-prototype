Dependent children assessment

To complete this section we need to know if you have any dependent children.

A dependent child is a person aged:

  • 0 to 15 in a household (whether they are in a family or not)
  • 16 to 18 in full-time education and living in a family with his or her parent or grandparent

We need this information to determine the student's eligibility.

Evidence you will need

Depending on your answers, you may need to upload copies of your child's:

  • Birth certificate
  • Foster carer letter
  • Adoption letter

If your child is aged 16 to 18 and in full time education we will need evidence of this.
