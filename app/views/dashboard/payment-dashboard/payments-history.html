Understanding your payments

This section splits your payments into the relevant terms for each academic year so you can view itemised amounts and a toal for each term. Each term will offer the following information:

    Type

    NHS Bursary element desciption

    Amount

    The amount due to you

    Expected date

    This is the date we anticipate we will make a payment based on your cohort and your attendance status being 'Matched student -in attendance'. This is only a predicted date and the actual date of payment is only confirmed once payment date is populated.
    Note This will not display for Travel and Dual Accomodation Expenses and Exceptional Support Fund payments. These payment types are issued immediately once approved, so will only display a payment date once this is confirmed.

    Payment date

    This is the actual date a payment will credit your nominated bank account. This will only populate when the true payment date has been confirmed via our payment release process.
