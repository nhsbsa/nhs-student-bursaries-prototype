Change of circumstance Before you start

It's important to let us know straight away if your circumstances change as this could affect how much NHS Bursary you're entitled to.

Examples of changes you need to tell us about include changes to your:

  • address
  • relationship status
  • bank details

You can find a longer list of changes on our Manage your NHS Bursary page (opens in a new tab).

We'll need to know:

  • what the change is
  • the date it happened
  • the reason it happened

You can also upload evidence to support your change of circumstance if you need to.

Information:

For your security, you'll be signed out if you do not do anything for 60 minutes.
