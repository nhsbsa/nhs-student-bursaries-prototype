Change of circumstance Before you start

Only tell us about a change if you think it will affect how much money you can get.

Examples of a change of circumstance could include:

  • moving out of your parent's home
  • getting married
  • having children
  • a change to your household income

These are only examples of a change of circumstance, you can tell us about other changes.

If you tell us about a change, we'll need to know:

  • what the change is
  • the date of the change
  • the reason for the change

You'll need to upload evidence of your change.

Information:

For your security, you will be signed out of the service if you do not do anything for 60 minutes.
