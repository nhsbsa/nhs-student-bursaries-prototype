Get help with childcare costs

You may be eligible for help with your childcare costs if you:

  • are receiving an {{ serviceName }}
  • have children under 15, or under 17 if they have special educational needs
  • pay for registered childcare while you attend your course

Your childcare allowance:

  • does not have to be paid back
  • is paid on top of your bursary

What you will get

You can get up to 85% of your childcare costs paid for while attending your course, but it will not cover all your costs.

The amount you'll get depends on:

  • your household income
  • the number of children who are dependent on you

The most you can get is:

  • up to £128.78 a week for 1 child
  • up to £191.45 a week for 2 or more children

You must pay for any remaining costs

Example

If your childcare for 1 child costs £100 a week, you’ll get £85 - this is 85% of your costs.

If your childcare for 1 child costs £220 a week, you’ll get £128.78. This is because 85% of your costs is £187, which is above the weekly limit.

Important:

Childcare allowance is not the same as 15 or 30 hours free childcare. You can’t use it to pay for those hours.

Eligibility

To claim for Childcare Allowance, all the following must apply:

  • You’re receiving an {{ serviceName }} (but not if you’re receiving an EU Fees only award)
  • Your child is under 15, or under 17 if they have special educational needs
  • The children in your application are financially dependent on you
  • are receiving care from an Ofsted-registered childcare provider
  • You (or your partner) are not claiming Tax-Free Childcare or childcare benefits from Working Tax Credit or Universal Credit

How you are paid

If you’re eligible for Childcare Allowance, you’ll be paid each term. Your payment will go into your bank account at the same time as your {{ serviceName }}.

Your childcare payments may change each term, as they are based on the weekly amounts you have entered in your application.

If your circumstances change

You must tell us if your childcare costs or providers change as this could affect what you’re entitled to. This will allow us to make sure that you receive the correct amount.

You can find out more about Childcare Allowance on our website.
